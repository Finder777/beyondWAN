DATA PRIVACY PROTOCOL

STATUS: ACTIVE | VERSION: 1.0.2026

01. DATA COLLECTION

We use Google Tag Manager (GTM) to analyze traffic. This protocol only initializes upon user authorization via the "ACCEPT" command on our interface.

02. COOKIE USAGE

  • _ga: Used to distinguish unique users.
  • cookie-consent: Stores your preference regarding tracking (Local Storage).

03. THIRD-PARTY INTERCEPT

Your data is never sold. We only use standard industry tools (GTM, Leaflet, Open-Meteo) to provide the LOCSTAT experience.

04. ENCRYPTION & SECURITY

All transmissions are handled via HTTPS. We prioritize the "No-Referrer" policy to prevent data leakage to external domains.
